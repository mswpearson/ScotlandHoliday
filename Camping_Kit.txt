Camping Kit for Scotland:
	- Tents	(Rory, Max, Matty)
	- Sleeping Bag	(Will still be quite cold overnight 5C so think about this)
	- Sleeping Mat
	- Pillow (if desired)
	- Head Torch
	- Camping Stove	(Ideally 2 between group)
	- Gloves
	- Tarp	(make shelter if wet and miserable)
	- Midge Spray
	- Portable Chargers
	- Chair
	- Food recepticle and eating utensils
	- Midge Net
