Things to bring for hills:
	- Day Bag 25-40L
	- Waterproofs	(ideally coat and trousers)
	- Sturdy Boots/Walking shoes
	- Sun Cream
	- Lots Water
	- Food
	- Hat (Sun+Cold)
	- Midge Spray
	- Tick Card
	- Some kind of survival bag/blanket
	- Copy of route	(ideally paper + electronic)
	- Torch
	- Blister Plasters + First Aid Kit
	- Camera	(if desired)
	- Sunglasses always nice
